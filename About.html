About the artist

Julien Leonard is a french generative artist specialized in vector dots paintings.

Using black dots as the sole graphical element, he designs and implements custom algorithms to create incredibly multiscaled complex yet coherent art.

Julien's art is meant to share with the viewer the wonder of Nature's organization.

Julien's work has been sold as commissions from private customers and organizations around the world.

He currently works in Singapore, inspired by the luxuriant Nature and the rich cultures of South East Asia.

Artist statement

"I create algorithms that connect dots. Lots of them. Only black.

Computers run those algorithms, assembling patterns of dots from a seed.

They grow images, for me to pick up and share.

Only dots can be used. And only dots that touch one another.

No collision, no isolation.

Only network and connection.

As molecules. As cells. As ourselves."
