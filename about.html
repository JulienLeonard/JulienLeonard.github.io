Bio and statement

Welcome and thanks for coming to this website !

I am Julien, a french software engineer exploring generative design since 2007.

I discovered generative design with fractals, spending hundred hours as a kid exploring the Mandelbrot set.

This led me to study computer science, and the discovery of the concept of emergence, where simple elements and local rules can produce designs with incredible and beautiful complexity.

I decided to use this concept as a basis for exploring a new kind of generative art, relying upon the rrangement of dots/

While I have been using different programming languages along the years (Python, Ruby, C++), my work is now based on C and Tcl for desktop, and vanilla Javascript with WebGL for the browser.
