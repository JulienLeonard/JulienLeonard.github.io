About the artist

Julien Leonard is a french generative artist specialized in vector dots paintings.

Using dots as the sole graphical element, he designs and implements custom algorithms to create art incredibly complex, multiscaled yet coherent.

Julien's work has been sold as commissions from private customers and organizations around the world.

He currently works in Singapore, inspired by the luxuriant equatorial vegetation and the rich cultures of South-East Asia.

Artist statement

I create algorithms that connect dots. Lots of them.

Computers run those algorithms, growing patterns of dots from a unique seed.

They produce images, for me to pick up and share.

Only dots can be used. And only dots that touch one another.

No collision, no isolation.

Only network and connection.

As molecules. As cells. As ourselves.\
