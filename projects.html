Alphabatiks

Alphabatik is a serie of works illustrating a new way to chain patterns of circles according to their ratios. A diversity of results are attained, with very often surprising complexity and aesthetics.

Algorigens

An ongoing study of works based on the bao algorithm, which consits in packing circles around each other in an envelopping movement. It produces designs similar to australian arborigen art, or water diffusion.

Digistreams

Project to assemble circles into strings to create patterns.

Maxircles

Project to circles packing reacting to their container.
